About

By The Word Counseling exists to provide biblical counseling for women that is accessible, compassionate, and faithful to the gospel. My approach is simple: open the Bible, listen carefully, apply truth, and walk with people toward hope in Christ.

  • Certified biblical counselor (ACBC)
  • Gospel-centered care for women
  • In-person and virtual sessions

What to Expect

Our process includes an initial meeting, a clear plan of care, and practical homework built around Scripture, prayer, and community.
